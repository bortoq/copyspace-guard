# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

Examples of expected behavior:

- respectful and constructive communication;
- openness to technical feedback;
- focus on facts, reproducibility, and collaboration.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or discrimination;
- personal attacks;
- deliberate disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and contributions that violate it.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact project maintainers via repository security/contact channels listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
